2. What data is processed?

When you use FuBot, the following information may be processed:

  • The passcode assigned to you for this study or service.
  • The questions you submit to the chatbot.
  • The answers the chatbot provides.
  • Timestamps and basic technical information required for operation and security.

3. Purpose of processing

The data is processed for research and evaluation purposes in the field of digital governance, and to improve the functioning and reliability of the chatbot.

4. Legal basis

The legal basis for processing is your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) and, where applicable, the performance of a task carried out in the public interest or in the exercise of official authority (Art. 6(1)(e) GDPR).

5. Storage period

Chat data and passcodes are stored for the duration of the research project and may be retained in anonymised or aggregated form for scientific analysis and publication. Direct identifiers are minimised as far as possible.

6. Data sharing

Data is not shared with third parties for commercial purposes. Research partners may receive access to anonymised or pseudonymised data for scientific analysis.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.
